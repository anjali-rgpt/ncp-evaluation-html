Academic Achievements

Student receives scholarship for merit

A student from Amrita School of Engineering, Ettimadai, has received the Chancellor's scholarship with regard to her academic merit. She has battled odds in her family and overcome hurdles to her education to reach here. She will be compensated for the entire tuition expenses and accommodation.

Computer Science Team wins Hackathon

A team from the Department of Computer Science has competed in the prestigious La Trobe Hackathon and won a grand prize. They have developed an innovative biomedical solution that tracks signals from the brain and detects emotion, stress and stability.
